The 6-Month Learning Roadmap

Weeks 1-8: Foundations

Phase 1

Start your journey by building a rock-solid base. This phase focuses on Python programming, data manipulation with Pandas, and the essential principles of statistics and mathematics that underpin all data science.

Weeks 9-15: Machine Learning

Phase 2

Dive into the core of data science. You'll learn the fundamentals of Machine Learning, including regression and classification, and then immediately apply them by building two complete, end-to-end projects ready for deployment.

Weeks 16-24: Specialization

Phase 3

Round out your skillset by mastering database querying with SQL, creating insightful dashboards with BI tools like Power BI, and exploring the advanced capabilities of Deep Learning for complex problems.
